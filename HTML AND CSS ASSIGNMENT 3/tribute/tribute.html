Mohan Das Karam Chand Gandhi

(1869-1948)

"An eye for eye only ends up making the whole world blind."

M.K.Gandhi was an anti-colonial nationalist and political ethicist who employed nonviolent resistance to lead the successful campaign for India's independence from British rule and in turn inspired movements for civil rights and freedom across the world.

  • Born: 2 October, 1869, Porbandar
  • Parents:Putlibai - Karamchand Gandhi
  • Spouse: Kasturba Gandhi (m. 1883-1944)
  • Grand-Children: Leela Gandhi
